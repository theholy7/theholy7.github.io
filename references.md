I have created this page to share links to articles that I have found meaningful.
Essentially, these are the articles that have helped me shape my engineering and my work beliefs.
Beliefs are not one-shot, they iterate and change over time.
My hope with this list is that I can track articles that resonated with me, and share them at the appropriate time with friends and colleagues.

Sadly, I should have started this as soon as I created my website. Many articles are now lost to time, and only a vague idea remains in my memory. Changing that starts today.
